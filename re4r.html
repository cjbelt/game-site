Will Resident Evil 4 Remake be a good game?

Coming in march 2023, Resident Evil 4 Remake is expected by many fans of the series. The remake will do a reimagination of the story, bringing a survival horror style to the game, rather than the action-focused atmosphere of the original one.

I do liked the idea of change the atmosphere of the game. Resident Evil 4 would be better if Capcom would bring more horror parts.

But there's a strange thing that I noticed: recently, Capcom released a 12 minute gameplay of the chapter 5 of the game, which Leon rescues Ashley from the church. But, wait... In the original game, we're in the island on chapter 5. Will Capcom cut some parts of the original game?

One of the main flaws of Resident Evil 3 Remake was the removing of big parts of the original game, like the clock tower. It's unlikely that Capcom will repeat the same error again. But I don't discard that chance.

If you are excited for the RE4 Remake, consider that possibility and don't expect too much for the game, for your mental health and your gamer experience.

The last game launches teached me to not illude myself with videogames. So, I consider every bad signal of a game to evaluate my expectations. But, anyway, I guess RE4 Remake will be a good game. The screenshots from the game are awesome!
